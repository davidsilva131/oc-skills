# Writing Shape

The **exploit** phase of writing: shape raw material into an article, paragraph by paragraph.

## Process

1. **Read the pile** — the full fragment document
2. **Establish prerequisites** — what does the reader need first?
3. **Draft 2-3 candidate openings**
4. **Grow paragraph by paragraph**: ask "what does the reader need to hear next?"
5. **Append as you go**
6. **Loop until done**

## Rules

- One paragraph at a time
- After each paragraph, re-read the full draft
- Push back on weak transitions — every paragraph must earn its place
- Argue format choices: prose vs list, inline vs callout, code vs description

## Out of scope

- Mining new fragments (use `/writing-fragments`)
- Editing raw material (do that by hand)
- Publishing or formatting
