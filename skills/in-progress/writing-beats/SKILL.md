# Writing Beats

The **exploit** phase of writing: shape raw material into a journey of beats.

## What is a beat

One move in the reader's journey. A single point, example, or transition.

## Process

1. **Establish prerequisites**: what concepts does the reader need to already understand?
2. **Write 2-3 candidate starting beats** — the first move
3. **Write only that beat to file**
4. **Offer 2-3 candidate next beats**
5. **Loop until natural end**

## Rules

- One beat at a time — never jump ahead
- Re-read the full article before each new beat
- Offer choices; let the user pick the direction
- A beat is done when it makes one clear point
